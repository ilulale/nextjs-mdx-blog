# Top 5 Korean Horror Movies

Korean cinema has a reputation for producing some of the best horror films in the world, and with good reason. From creepy ghost stories to intense zombie outbreaks, these movies will have you on the edge of your seat. Here are our picks for the top 5 Korean horror movies:

1. **"The Wailing" (2016)** - This highly acclaimed horror film tells the story of a small village that is plagued by a mysterious illness, and a police officer who becomes embroiled in the supernatural occurrences while trying to solve the case.   
![The Wailing poster](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcSrYMrAmbQzyvHHZYOUJsO9UCvZYlsDQwE8iw&usqp=CAU)

2. **"Train to Busan" (2016)** - This film follows a group of passengers on a high-speed train as they fight for survival against a sudden outbreak of zombies. With intense action and heart-wrenching drama, "Train to Busan" has become a fan favorite in the horror genre.  
![Train to Busan poster](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcSk0mnJ8lDyouJcczmQY_mkBduLuEqR1D2oKQ&usqp=CAU)

3. **"The Host" (2006)** - This monster movie follows a family as they try to rescue their daughter after she is abducted by a mutant creature that emerges from the Han River. With a mix of horror and comedy, "The Host" is a must-see for fans of the genre.   
![The Host poster](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQUiMN3UJG2y4bsTRUnBWf82wH3jvg8lN0EhA&usqp=CAU)

4. **"A Tale of Two Sisters" (2003)** - This psychological thriller tells the story of two sisters who return to their family home after a stint in a mental hospital, only to find that the house is haunted by the ghosts of their deceased mother and father.   
![A Tale of Two Sisters poster](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcTprhUlbLFf5Pd0lJjSu3_tYqAZEv5MuF58Qg&usqp=CAU)

5. **"Thirst" (2009)** - This vampire film follows a Catholic priest who becomes a vampire after receiving a blood transfusion, and must navigate his new undead existence while struggling with his faith and desire for blood. With its unique blend of horror and romance, "Thirst" is a standout in the Korean horror scene.   
![Thirst poster](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/b/b1/Thirstposter.jpg)

If you're a fan of horror movies, these five films are a great place to start exploring the rich and varied world of Korean horror cinema.
