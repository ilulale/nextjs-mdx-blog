# Classic Rock Songs

Classic rock is a genre of music that has stood the test of time and continues to be a fan favorite to this day. Here are the top five classic rock songs:

1. [Stairway to Heaven by Led Zeppelin](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oW_7vEKpIWc) - Iconic guitar intro and soaring vocals.
2. [Bohemian Rhapsody by Queen](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fJ9rUzIMcZQ) - Complex and epic song with operatic vocals and guitar solo.
3. [Sweet Child o' Mine by Guns N' Roses](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1w7OgIMMRc4) - Catchy guitar riffs and emotive lyrics.
4. [Hotel California by The Eagles](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EqPtz5qN7HM) - Iconic guitar intro and memorable lyrics.
5. [Black Dog by Led Zeppelin](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yR2YTWW4cJ4) - Upbeat rocker with memorable guitar riff and powerful vocals.

##### These are just a few of the many classic rock songs that have become anthems and have left a lasting impact on the music industry. Be sure to check out our website for more recommendations on classic rock and other genres of music.
